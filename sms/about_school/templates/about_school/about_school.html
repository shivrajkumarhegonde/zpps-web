About Our ZPPS Kavathe School

ZPPS Kavathe was established in 1938 and is managed by the local body. It is located in a picturesque rural area, specifically in the Solapur North block of Solapur district, Maharashtra. The school consists of grades from 1 to 7 and is co-educational, creating a balanced and inclusive environment for all students.

The medium of instruction at this school is Marathi, promoting local culture and language. The school operates on a regular schedule with the academic session starting in April. The school has a government building with the following outstanding facilities:
  • • 7 well-maintained classrooms for instructional purposes
  • • 2 additional rooms for non-teaching activities
  • • A dedicated room for the headmaster/teacher
  • • A secure boundary wall
  • • Reliable electric connection
  • • Functional tap water as a source of drinking water
  • • A spacious playground for sports and recreational activities
  • • A library with a collection of 250 books, fostering a love for reading

The school takes pride in its vibrant extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and cultural events, providing a holistic education experience. Our dedicated staff and teachers are committed to nurturing the potential of each student, ensuring they excel academically and personally.

Additionally, the school provides nutritious mid-day meals prepared on the premises, ensuring the health and well-being of our students. We strive to create a safe, supportive, and enriching environment where every child can thrive.
